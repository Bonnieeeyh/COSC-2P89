Frequently Asked Questions


1. What is BonniesHandmadeShop.ca? +

Bonnie's Handmade Shop is a creative space dedicated to bringing unique, handcrafted items to life. I specialize in handmade phone cases, wands, personalized phone stands, and more. Each item is thoughtfully crafted with a focus on quality and creativity.

Whether you have a specific idea in mind or want to explore custom designs, my shop is here to help you find or create the perfect piece! Feel free to reach out with ideas or questions, and let’s craft something truly special together!

2. How can I place the order? +

To place an order, simply follow these steps:

   a. Browse our products and select the item(s) you wish to purchase.

   b. Add the selected products to your shopping cart. (If for customization, please contact with me)

   c. Proceed to checkout, where you will provide shipping and payment information.

   d. Review your order and complete the payment process.

   e. Once the order is confirmed, I will process and ship to your provided address.

3. Do you offer free shipping? +

Free shipping is available for orders over $100. For orders below that amount, a standard shipping fee will apply.

4. How do I place a custom order? +

Simply contact me through email with your design ideas or preferences, then we will discuss the details for your custom products.

5. Do you offer customization for all products? +

Yes, I provide full customization for all my products! You can send me your favorite figures, images, or just tell me your preferences, and I will create a special design only for you.

6. Can I return or exchange products? +

Since all products are handmade, I currently do not accept returns or exchanges. If you encounter any issues, please reach out, and I will try my best to offer help.

7. How can I track my order? +

Once your order is shipped, I will send you a confirmation email containing a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can use this information to monitor the status of your delivery.

8. How long will it take to receive my handmade items? +

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the selected shipping method. After placing your order, I will provide you with a tracking number to monitor the status of your shipment.

9. Can I cancel my order before shipping? +

If you wish to cancel your order, please contact me as soon as possible to request a cancellation before the shipping process begins.

However, if your order is a custom order, once it is placed, cancellation is not possible due to the personalized nature of the product.
